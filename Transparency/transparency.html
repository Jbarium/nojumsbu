Transparency Policy

Welcome to the Transparency Policy page of the SBU Astronomy Association. We believe in maintaining a transparent and open environment for our members and stakeholders. Our transparency policy outlines our commitment to providing accurate and timely information to our members and the public.

Transparency Guidelines

  • Be open and honest in all communications.
  • Provide accurate information to members and the public.
  • Share financial reports and updates regularly.
  • Disclose decision-making processes and governance structures.

Transparency Initiatives

At the SBU Astronomy Association, we regularly organize meetings and discussions with our members to keep them informed about the association's activities and future plans. We encourage active participation and feedback from all our members to ensure a transparent decision-making process.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our transparency policy or any other matter related to the association, please don't hesitate to contact us. We value your input and are committed to transparency and accountability.
